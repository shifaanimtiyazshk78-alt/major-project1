About OCPMS

Learn about our platform, vision, and how we're transforming courier operations.

One Platform. Complete Control.

The Online Courier & Parcel Management System integrates customer-facing services, internal operational workflows, and administrative management into a single, cohesive platform.

It replaces manual, paper-intensive processes with a digital, efficient, and transparent ecosystem—enhancing the experience for customers, delivery personnel, and management alike.

The Existing System

The current operational model in many small to mid-sized courier companies is predominantly manual or relies on disparate, non-integrated tools—leading to numerous inefficiencies.

📋

Manual Booking & Records

Ledgers/Excel, prone to errors and loss.

📞

Inefficient Tracking

Phone-based, no central real-time view.

📄

Paper-Based Waybills

Physical slips, unreliable audit trails.

🔀

Disconnected Communication

No proactive updates, poor visibility.

Our Proposed System

A unified, role-based web application accessible via any modern browser—overhauling the existing model.

🗄️

Centralized Database

Single, secure storage for all data—users, shipments, transactions.

⚡

Automated Workflow

Defined parcel lifecycle with automatic notifications.

📸

Digital POD

Capture signatures/photos directly from devices.

Our Objectives

Creating a seamless bridge between customers, courier staff, and administrators.

Digitize Operations — Fully digital workflow for booking, tracking, delivery.
Enhance Customer Experience — 24/7 portal, real-time tracking, automated notifications.
End-to-End Tracking — Unique Tracking IDs, real-time status for all stakeholders.
Optimize Delivery Management — Dedicated module for field staff with digital POD.
Automate Pricing & Invoicing — Dynamic pricing, electronic invoices and receipts.
